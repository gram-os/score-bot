How to use this

The suggested multipliers are a starting point based on historical averages. To apply them, you would store a difficulty_multiplier per game and multiply base_score × multiplier before adding speed bonuses. Consider recalibrating periodically as more submissions come in, and check whether any games have too few submissions to give a reliable estimate (look for small counts above).
